Swallow Crest Farm Website Project

Sitemap

Contained within these four-pointed walls of HTML is my Swallow Crest Farm website. Full of fun and amusement, crafted to the client's needs, ensuring a responsive and interactive environment.

It features a total of nine pages the home, blog, distribution, CSA distribution, subscription, blog, events, recipes, and contact pages, each incorporating a section from the provided material given by the client in the discovery phase.

The website boasts a green, blue, and white color scheme to match the colors featured on the Swallow Crest Logo. Also included are several widgets used to enhance user interactions with the recipes and events page and a plethora of plugins allowing for ease of use features such as event calendars, forms, theme editors, galleries, and image optimization.
