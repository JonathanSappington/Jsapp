Form Links

www.formassembly.com

A site that focuses on the pitfalls of form design, emphasizing user convenience and proper formatting for questionnaires. The website had a handful of helpful tips, from input box size to more efficient formatting for text boxes like phone numbers and state names. It helped me visualize just how much effort and thought is needed when designing a form properly.

www.particletree.com

A helpful page about ten tips for better form design, showcasing common tricks that developers tend to miss when working with HTML. I found a few interesting alternative form format elements like Mini Calendar and visual mapper making life a bit easier when dealing with more complex form elements.
